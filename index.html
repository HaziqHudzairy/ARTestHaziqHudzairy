LANDMARK BASED AR IN UNIVERSITY MALAYA: UM.AR


Augmented Reality (AR) technology has gained significant interest in recent years due to its potential to enhance user experiences in various domains. In line with this, my proposed project aims to leverage AR technology to create an immersive campus exploration and navigation system for students at University Malaya.
